In order to determine how to write a paragraph, you will have to find a good topic and collect enough information regarding the topic. Once you find the supporting details, you can start framing the sentences, connect the sentences following a sequence, and find a perfect concluding sentence. To understand it better, we have provided a few paragraph writing examples for your reference. Find a Topic Sentence: It is the first sentence which is an introduction to the given topic. It gives the main idea of what the paragraph would be about. Supporting details: These are the details that can be collected from various sources. It comprises information related to the topic that gives strong support to the main topic. Closing sentence: It is the last sentence that ends the paragraph and restates the whole idea of the paragraph. It is basically the concluding sentence that gives the basic idea of the whole topic.
